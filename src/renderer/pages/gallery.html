Letter to Blumentritt

Correspondence with his closest friend, Austrian scholar Ferdinand Blumentritt. Their letters discussed Philippine history, culture, languages, and politics. This friendship exemplified intellectual exchange across cultures and Rizal's ability to build bridges between East and West.

Rizal's scholarly annotations to Antonio de Morga's 1609 historical work "Sucesos de las Islas Filipinas." These annotations corrected Spanish biases and highlighted the advanced civilization of pre-colonial Philippines, reclaiming Filipino heritage and dignity.
